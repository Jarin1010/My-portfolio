About Me

I am Jarin Tchicaya, originally from the Republic of Congo and I have been in the United States for four years. My hobbies are going to taekwondo practice, going out with friends and traveling.

I am currently taking a multidisciplinary approach to attaining proficiency in design thinking, product management, user interface design, and web design. I am seeking to graduate in August 2021.

“My design philosophy are
Observation, Creativity and Design.”

I am a huge believer of innovation because It has brought the world to where it is now with all its inventions. In everything that I do, I always bring the spirit of innovation to make things better.
